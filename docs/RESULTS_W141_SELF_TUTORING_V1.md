- **`coordpy.self_tutoring_technique_extractor_v1`** — AST-derives a leak-audited holed skeleton from a concrete winning program. `correct_fill` = the winner's ORIGINAL blanked expressions ⇒ completable BY CONSTRUCTION; `trivial_fill` = constant stubs ⇒ `holes_are_substantive` bites. Every extraction is cleared by the EXISTING `tutor_leak_gate_v1` (new `spec_override`) + a completability re-run; on any failure the caller DISCARDS → KEEP ≡ A1 (non-negativity structural).
- **`coordpy.no_oracle_verifier_v1`** — S1 (differential agreement with a SELF-WRITTEN brute on self-generated small inputs, skipping inputs where the brute itself crashes) ∧ S2 (worst-case dual-large-input timeout; rejects the slow brute AND distribution shortcuts). Commits by output-signature consensus among winners; ABSTAINs on none/tie (the supply pre-screen).
- **`coordpy.self_tutoring_controller_v1`** — DISCOVER (self-brute + K diverse + constraint-adversarial bank + verify → winner → extract) → AMORTIZE (apply the one scaffold to held-out members) + the earn gate. The S1 bank is the model-generated constraint-binding generator exec'd in the audited subprocess (the documented W125-escape).
