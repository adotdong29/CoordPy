# Frontier-Relevance Audit — W142 (moderate-`p` family screen + discover-reliability)

**Question this audit answers (the programme's standing discipline):** is the W142 result frontier-relevant, or a toy artifact? W142 measures, on the frontier anchor `meta/llama-3.3-70b-instruct`, whether ≥3 resistant-by-construction algorithmic families live in the moderate-`p` band where no-oracle self-tutoring amortization converts a single coordination win into a family-spanning same-budget superiority.

## 1. The anchor is frontier-grade and the exact retirement model
The screen + pilot run on `meta/llama-3.3-70b-instruct` — the **exact model that earned the W105 retirement** (+7.00pp on HumanEval+). It is a current frontier-class open-weight model. A same-budget multi-agent superiority on it is a frontier claim by the programme's own established standard, not a small-model curiosity. (Cross-scale to Maverick is an optional, separate check per RUNBOOK §9.)

## 2. Resistant-by-construction answers the contamination confound directly
The W108–W121 arc established that official code benchmarks at 70B are either contamination-EXPOSED (the W89/W105 retirements live there) or, when contamination-RESISTANT, supply-capped / saturated (W120–W123). W132 minted a resistant-BY-CONSTRUCTION field (fresh, parser-neutral, exact-oracle-graded, novelty-guarded) so the contamination axis is removed by construction, not by date-filtering. W142 inherits that field (`make_pn_template` discipline: exact-oracle `ref` / independent `brute` / admissible-wrong `naive` / parser-neutral I/O / novelty guard). So a W142 win cannot be memorization — the families are minted fresh and surface-disguised.

## 3. The moderate-`p` band is exactly where a frontier model is informative
The IRT result (metabench arXiv:2407.12844; Fluid Benchmarking arXiv:2509.11106) is that item/technique discrimination is maximal at `p≈0.5` and zero at the saturated (`p≈1`) and dead (`p≈0`) extremes. The W142 screen targets `p∈[0.10,0.50]` precisely because that is where the frontier model is *partially capable* — it sometimes-but-not-reliably writes the efficient technique. A mechanism that converts partial capability into reliable family-level success at matched compute is a frontier-relevant coordination capability, not an exploit of a model weakness. The fair-neutral baseline (no technique/efficiency cue, FormatSpread arXiv:2310.11324 robustness over a prompt bank) ensures the measured `p` is the model's genuine intrinsic rate, not a cue artifact.

## 4. The mechanism is a general coordination capability, not benchmark-specific
No-oracle discover-once-amortize-many (DreamCoder arXiv:2006.08381 / Voyager arXiv:2305.16291 transposed to oracle-free LLM code-gen; distinct from RLAD arXiv:2510.02263's per-problem oracle-warm-started abstractions) is a capability any agentic coding system can use on any family of same-technique problems. The W142 battlefield is the *measurement instrument*, not the claim; the claim is that a multi-agent team can self-derive a technique from its own rare correct sample and transfer it — a frontier-relevant property of coordination, audited to be no-oracle and leak-clean.

## 5. Honest limitation (the audit's adverse finding)
The field is CONSTRUCTED, not an official benchmark with external prestige. This is a deliberate trade (official benchmarks are contaminated/saturated at 70B — the W108–W121 finding), but it means the result's external validity rests on the construction discipline (parser-neutrality, exact-oracle discrimination, novelty guard, no-leakage, fair-`p`) rather than on a recognized leaderboard. The audit's standing requirement: the result is frontier-relevant **iff** the screen's $0 gates and fair-`p` discipline hold — which they are machine-checked to (G1–G5 + the no-oracle audit). A second adverse finding the audit flags up front: the moderate-`p` ∧ extractable ∧ span-distinct intersection may be intrinsically THIN at this model scale (the bimodal-generation wall), in which case the honest outcome is a NAMED supply cap, not a retirement — and that cap is itself a frontier-relevant statement about the 70B's bimodal technique distribution.

## Verdict
**Frontier-relevant, conditional on the construction discipline.** The anchor is frontier-grade and the exact retirement model; contamination is removed by construction; the moderate-`p` target is the IRT-informative band; the mechanism is general. The result (earn or cap) is a frontier-relevant statement either way, provided the $0 gates + fair-`p` + no-oracle audit pass (they do). Not a toy; not over-claimed.
