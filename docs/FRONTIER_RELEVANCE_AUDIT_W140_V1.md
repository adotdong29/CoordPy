## Anti-patterns (REAFFIRMED — the critical boundary)

**Bounded context / compaction / prose-summary / "cram less, truncate better" REMAIN explicit anti-patterns, and W140 SHARPENS why.** A family-level tutor is the OPPOSITE of context compression: it is a *compilation of a winning mechanism* (the anchor's execution-grounded witness) into a denser, oracle-grounded, family-level TEACHING object — it ADDS structured technique knowledge a weak model lacks, it does not truncate or summarize the model's context. The W140 tutor:
- carries MORE signal than the bare diagnostic (technique name + worked scaffold + oracle routing), not less;
- is leakage-audited to teach a transferable technique, not to shrink a prompt;
- is gated by a measured per-ability tutor-usability prior, not applied as a generic compression.
Any framing of W140 as "better context compression" or "summarize the witness" is a category error and is explicitly rejected. The mechanism is teaching-compilation, a coordination primitive (R6′), not a context-window trick.
